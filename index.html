Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • They change colour to communicate
  • Some Chameleons can grow up to 27 inches
  • Chameleons have 360 degree vision

Interesting Facts About Camels

  • Camels slepp standing up
  • Camels can run up to 40mph
  • Camels can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities
  • They can find their nest from 1300 miles away
  • Pigeons understand time

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Pandas can weigh between 75kg and 135kg
  • They eat from 25 to 40 pounds per day
  • Pandas do not hibernate

Interesting Facts About Lions

  • An adult male’s roar can be heard up to 8km away
  • Lions run at a speed of up to 81kmph
  • Lions can eat up to 40kg of meat in a single meal

Interesting Facts About Gorillas

  • Humans share around 98% of DNA with Gorillas
  • Gorillas can live over 40 years old
  • Gorillas are mainly vegetarian
